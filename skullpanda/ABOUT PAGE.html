About

Artist

熊喵：When I designed Skullpanda,
I designed it with the mindset of "this is the other me",
but that doesn't mean I want to be Skullpanda.

The design of Skullpanda may bring in my perceptions and
exploration of some stories in my life,
which are complementary to the expression of my spirituality.

Skullpanda is set as a girl with a personality that can
travel freely in the universe.
It has the helmet of an astronaut and spherical braids.
I hope it will be bold and self-explanatory enough to have
the opportunity to experience all kinds of life
and encounter exciting or bumpy stories.
