Honey Garlic Chicken Wings

Info

  • Servings: 2/3
  • Time to prepare: 3 hours

Ingredients

  • 12 Chicken wings
  • 0.5 spoon of Dark soy sauce
  • 2 spoon of Light soy sauce
  • 2 spoon of Cooking wine
  • 1 spoon of Oyster sauce
  • 2.5 spoon of Honey (Add more if you like)
  • 2 spoon of Minced garlic (Add more if you like)
  • a few black pepper

Instructions

  1. Wash and cut a few small slits on the opposite side of the chicken wings
  2. Pour all of the ingredients into a bowl and make a marinade.
  3. Place the chicken wings in the marinade for at least one hour.
  4. Pre-heat the air fryer at 370F for 5 mins
  5. Lay the tinfoil in the air fryer
  6. Bake the wings at 330 for 25 minutes, turning once halfway through. (Tip: Adjust the temperature and time according to your air fryer.)
  7. Sprinkle some white sesame on top and serve
